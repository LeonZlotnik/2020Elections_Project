Changes in CHIP enrollment 2017-2019

Over the last two years changes in policy have left a significant number newly enrolled youths without access to healthcare. With OBAMACARE undr pressure, which states are more vulnerable and how to ensure access for the poorest sectors of the population? This project allows the user to track the monthly changes in the numbers of enrolled citizens between 2017 and 2019 across America.
